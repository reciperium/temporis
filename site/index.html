Instructions

How to Open an Unsigned App on macOS
  1. Drag the app from the DMG into your Applications folder.
  2. Go to the Applications folder.
  3. Right-click (or Control-click) the app icon and select Open.
  4. In the pop-up window, click the Open button to run the app.
You only need to do this once. After that, you can open the app normally.

Focus Better, Live Better

Temporis has been crafted by consolidating my favorite features from different pomodoro apps into a single, native application that follows your operating system's design.

Focus & Diffusion

Switch between deep focus work and mind-wandering breaks to enhance creativity and avoid mental blocks.

Lightning fast

Built with performance in mind, without draining resources. Minimal impact on system resources. Designed for native performance.

Native Design

Beautiful interface that follows your operating system's design language, providing a seamless experience across different platforms.

Clever Notifications

Gentle reminders to stretch, hydrate, and move. Includes optional tick sounds and progress indicators.

Customizable

You can adjust intervals, notification preferences, and behavior to match your workflow.

Focus Mode (Planned)

Automatically activates "Do Not Disturb" mode during focus sessions (KDE support via D-Bus).
